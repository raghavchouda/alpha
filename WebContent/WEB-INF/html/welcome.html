Know About us?

SINCE 2005 Raghav Garden is one of the beautiful and largest marriage garden in Sagar situated at Rajeev Nagar. We provide well maintained destination for marriage, party and general recreation activities. The Garden is spread over 90 thousand sq. ft. of lush green eco-friendly well maintained land. Garden has huge parking facility for more than 100 cars. We have 12 King Size Ac Rooms , with 8000 Sq. Ft. of hall(2) capacity of 300 people. Thus Garden is well protected to stop ruin your function from the inconsistent rains in between. We have 24 hours supply of water, electricity backup and cctv surveillance. We also have Kids play zone.
